<objective>
Add reader coverage and safe artifact decisions for dictionary, run-end, and
sequence-like primitive Vortex shapes.
</objective>

<context>
Loom already has `LayoutNode::Dictionary` and `LayoutNode::RunEnd` semantics.
Phase 21 must determine whether real Vortex dictionary/run-end/sequence facts
can safely map to those Loom-owned nodes or should remain canonicalized,
interpreter-only, or fail-closed.
</context>

<threat_model>
- Threat: dictionary codes or run ends are accepted without bounds checks.
  Mitigation: require artifact verifier acceptance and negative coverage.
- Threat: canonicalized raw emission is mistaken for native dictionary/run-end
  lowering support.
  Mitigation: coverage facts must record original Vortex shape and lowering
  disposition separately from emitted Loom shape.
- Threat: Vortex APIs cannot construct or expose these shapes deterministically.
  Mitigation: record API gaps and fail closed rather than synthesizing false
  evidence.
</threat_model>
