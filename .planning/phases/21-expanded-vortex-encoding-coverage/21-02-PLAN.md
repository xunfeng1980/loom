<objective>
Add the first expanded Vortex coverage slice: nullable primitive facts and
chunked/split primitive facts, with explicit emission and lowering disposition.
</objective>

<context>
Phase 18 accepts only non-null primitive rows. Nullable primitives and
chunked/split layouts are the lowest-risk pressure tests because they force a
clear separation between reader facts, artifact emission, interpreter decode,
and Phase 20 all-valid raw-copy native lowering.
</context>

<threat_model>
- Threat: nullable rows are silently emitted as all-valid data.
  Mitigation: require validity oracle tests and keep emission unsupported unless
  Loom artifact validity is represented and verified.
- Threat: chunked data is emitted with unstable row order.
  Mitigation: require Vortex scan oracle equivalence and deterministic row order
  before any artifact emission.
- Threat: Phase 20 lowering treats nullable/chunked shapes as raw primitive
  native-supported.
  Mitigation: lowering disposition must remain `interpreter-only` or
  `fail-closed/deferred` unless the production lowering gate is explicitly
  extended and tested.
</threat_model>
