<objective>
Separate structured Vortex encoding semantics from canonical raw compatibility.

Purpose: stop canonicalized dictionary/run-end/bitpack/FOR fixtures from being
mistaken for original structured semantic support or native support.
Output: structured encoding tests, explicit deferral reasons, and gate checks.
</objective>

<threat_model>
T-30-04: Structured semantic overclaim. Canonical scan rows may be equivalent
at values while losing original code/value/run-end/bit-width/reference semantics.
Mitigation: matrix rows must carry original-shape and emitted-shape separately,
with stable deferral reasons for unavailable structured facts.
</threat_model>
