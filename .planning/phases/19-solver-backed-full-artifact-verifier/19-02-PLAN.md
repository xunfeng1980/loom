<objective>
Convert Loom constraints and proof obligations into deterministic replayable SMT-LIB scripts, with `QF_BV` as the required Bitwuzla-primary path.
</objective>

<context>
Current `ConstraintSet::to_smtlib_comments()` is diagnostic text only. Phase 19 needs executable SMT-LIB scripts that represent bad-state queries and can be run by the Bitwuzla backend.
</context>

<threat_model>
- Threat: QF_LIA accidentally remains the required path.
  Mitigation: make `QF_BV` emission the tested default for required obligations.
- Threat: generated scripts drift and become unreplayable.
  Mitigation: stable ordering, symbol hygiene, and snapshot-style tests.
- Threat: overflow is hidden by bit-vector wrap.
  Mitigation: encode overflow as part of the bad-state query, not as a benign operation.
</threat_model>
