<objective>
Create the production native-lowering gate that consumes unified artifact reports and rejects every non-discharged, unsupported, or under-specified artifact before any MLIR/native artifact can be emitted.
</objective>

<context>
Phase 14/16 lowering currently checks only `verify_l2_core` plus `VerifiedArtifactFacts` for the bounded Int32 copy slice. Phase 19 added solver-backed artifact facts. Phase 20 needs a stricter production-facing gate over `ArtifactVerificationReport`, not a bypass around it.
</context>

<threat_model>
- Threat: production lowering trusts `CollectedOnly` constraints.
  Mitigation: require `ConstraintDischargeStatus::Discharged` or `NotRequired`; all other statuses reject.
- Threat: accepted structural artifacts lower without associated L2/native facts.
  Mitigation: require accepted artifact facts, lowering-ready facts, row bound, and supported payload/kernel shape before support is true.
- Threat: the old bounded-copy support predicate becomes confused with production support.
  Mitigation: introduce a separate production module/report vocabulary and keep Phase 14 support stable.
</threat_model>
